What's AS Modal Dialogs?

It's a AngularJS module that gives you directives for easily create modal dialogs in your web application.

Why use it?

There are a lot of different situations which require a modal dialog, and sometimes it's difficult and laborious to create a special modal dialog for each one. AS Modal Dialogs gives you a easy way to create modal dialogs for everything. And you can also personalize it with your own css styles!
